CHAPTER THREE

The Beginning

Every love story begins with a moment that seems ordinary at the time. Ours started on **St. Philomena Day, August 2021**. It was just a college photoshoot—nothing more. We weren't in love; we were simply two people who started talking a little more than before. Then came Instagram. I edited one of our photos and needed your number to send it. One message became another, Instagram turned into WhatsApp, WhatsApp turned into late-night calls, and somehow those "just five more minutes" conversations became hours. Between sharing food, laughing at the smallest things, helping each other through difficult days, and wondering if we were truly meant to be each other's life partner, our story quietly wrote itself. We never realised exactly when "you and me" became "us." It just... happened. ❤️
